- The brain is the body's most complex organ and also the most complex biological structure in the known universe
- Neurons communicate via both electrical and chemical signals
- Genetically determined neural circuits are the foundation of the nervous system
- Neural plasticity: life experiences change the structure of the nervous system
- Intelligence arises as the brain reasons, plans, and solves problems
- The brain makes it possible to communicate knowledge via language
- The brain gives us curiosity to understand how the world works
