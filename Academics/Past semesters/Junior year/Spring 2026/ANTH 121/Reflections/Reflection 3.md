**Cusco**
- Ancient city in Peru
- Founded in 1100s as the Inka capital
- Conquered in 1500s by Spain
- **Major cities**
	- **Qorikancha**
		- “Temple of the Sun” in Cusco
			- Decorated with gold and riches
			- Large central church 
				- Known for remarkable architecture and engineering
				- Most important religious site in the Inka empire
				- Dedicated to sun god Inti
				- Site of Inti Raymi 
		- Ceque paths led to Qorikancha
		- Spaniards built over Inka architecture
	- **Sacsayhuaman**
		- Not pronounced “sexy woman” …allegedly
		- Ancient complex near Cusco
			- Built by the Inka in the 1400s
				- Constructed with limestone without mortar
			- Likely served as an observatory or religious center
