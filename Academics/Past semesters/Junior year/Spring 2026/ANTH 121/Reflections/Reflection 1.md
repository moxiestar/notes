**Chaco**
- Ancient settlement inhabited by Ancestral Puebloans 
- Existed from roughly 850-1250
- Located in the four corners region of the Southwest US
	- Ideal archaeological conditions
		- Dry desert area (good for preservation)
		- Lots of lumber
			- Tree ring dating 
			- Radiocarbon dating

**Chaco culture**
- Intensive maize agriculture 
- Great houses (over 300 found)
	- Large subterranean circular rooms
- Similar pottery, roads, and rock art

**Chaco Canyon**
- Downtown Chaco
- 12 great houses concentrated in a close area
- Very little evidence of production
- Many imports (chocolate, maize, jade)
