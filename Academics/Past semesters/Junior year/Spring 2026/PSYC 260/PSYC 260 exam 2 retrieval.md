(2006): threatening face & facial recognition  | Participants were shown a variety of faces (familiar/unfamiliar, negative/positive)<br>Negative faces, regardless of familiarity, had the highest salience                                                                                       |                                                                                                                          |

| **Actor-observer effect**                                      | Tendency to think that others’ behavior is caused by personal factors, while ours is caused by the situation                                                                                                                                     |                                                                                                                          |
