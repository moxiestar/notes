# **Credit process (manual)**

**Pipeline: gathering information -> getting reports -> making a decision**

1. Meet with customer and getting all relevant details (with permission)
2. Details then get printed out and sent to individual credit managers
3. Request financial statement (if delay till harvest is needed)

**Issues**

- No true scoring system/matrix - decisions often made based on word of mouth
- Amount of analog information can make it difficult to make a decision
- Lack of objective measures of customer reliability and responsibility
- Decisions can be made personally/subjectively
