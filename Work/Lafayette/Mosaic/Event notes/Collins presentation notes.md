# **Collins | Brand agency (NYC, SF, Woods Hole)**

**What is brand?**

- Brand is a promise performed consistently over time
- Brand is differentiation
- Brand should be specific and clear and effective

**Key components of branding**

- Familiarity (reassuring, iconic, retained)
- Surprising (ignorable, timely, recruiting)

**What does Collins do?**

- Help companies move from old game to new game

**Three act playbook for startups (Mike Vernal)**

- Old playbook: unbundling -> suite -> platform
- Now, ambition is more important than anything else
- “If you’re gonna go for it, you should probably just go for the whole thing”
- Always think of the more ambitious version of your idea and push towards achieving it

**Lulu’s advice for levels of company storytelling**

- First level: what are you doing?
- Second level: what does that represent?
- Third level: what is the shape and size of the dent in the universe if you succeed?

**Collins & Spotify**

- Spotify’s pitch: world’s best engineered music streaming service (really?)
- Spotify reached out to Collins in the early 2010s when their branding was still techy and European
- Collins knew branding should focus on music, not tech
- Took inspiration from the Fillmore West (contrasting pop art graphics)

**AI & creativity**

- Very little investment going into AI’s variability and creativity
- AI needs to be consistent and reliable over time, which decreases its individuality and creativity

**Note:** it’s easy to build a good story out of a great product, it’s hard to build a good story out of a bad product, so focus on building a good product
